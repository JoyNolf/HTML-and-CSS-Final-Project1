What we love

Penguins are classified as birds, though they are flightless.


They are waddling, lovable creatures that have knees, but no hips.

Where they are

Penguins prefer cold areas. They are often found near the poles of the globe.


Today, they can also be seen in zoos.

Fun Fact

A group of Penguins on land are called a waddle.


A group of Penguins in the water are called a raft.
